Blueberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

For crust
  • 2 1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
    • For filling
  • 4 8-ounce packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 16-ounce container sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
    • For topping
  • 2 6-ounce baskets fresh blueberries
  • 1/3 cup all-fruit blueberry spread

    • Preparation

    Make crust
    1. Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 375 F.
    2. Wrap outside of 10-inch-diameter springform an with heavy-duty foil.
    3. Combine all ingredients in processor and blend using on/off turns until crumbs are moist.
    4. Press crumb mixture firmly unto bottom and 2 inches up sides of prepared pan.
    5. Bake until crust begins to brown, about 8 minutes, and transfer crust to rack and cool.
